About

Steady v2.1

A gentle, private mood journal for noticing how you're doing over time.

Steady is a journaling tool, not a medical device or crisis service. It doesn't diagnose or treat anything, and it isn't a substitute for care from a doctor or therapist.

Everything you write stays on this device. If you turn on sync, it’s also stored privately in your own Steady account — and nowhere else. Photos never leave this device either way.
